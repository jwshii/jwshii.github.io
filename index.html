Jessica Shi

I am a PhD student in computer science at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Benjamin Pierce and Andrew Head. My research interest is proof assistant usability. I am a member of the programming languages group.

I completed my bachelor's degree at MIT, where I double majored in mathematics and computer science. I benefited from the undergraduate research program, including and especially as part of Saman Amarasinghe's compilers group.

I can be reached at jwshi@seas.upenn.edu.
